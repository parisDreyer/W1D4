How to cook pasta

Description

This recipe is how to cook your pasta properly. We will have ingredients listed and a list of directions on what to do.

Directions

  1. Boil the water
  2. Place noodles in boiling water
  3. Strain noodles from water and let sit
  4. Pour tomato sauce over.
